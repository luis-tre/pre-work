John Legend

BIO

Grammy and Academy Award-winning singer-songwriter John Legend is known for such acclaimed albums as 'Get Lifted' and 'Love in the Future. John Roger Stephens was born on December 28, 1978, in Springfield, Ohio. A child prodigy, Legend's grandmother taught him how to play the piano, and he grew up singing in the church choir. He went on to attend the University of Pennsylvania, where he directed a co-ed a cappella group. After graduation, he switched gears and worked for Boston Consulting Group, but continued to perform in nightclubs in New York City.
